About Me

I joined Missouri State University, United States. as an Assistant Professor of Computer Science in January 2024. Prior to this, I worked as a postdoctoral research associate at University of York, United Kingdom with Prof. Andy Tyrrell from July 2021 to Dec 2023. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Nevada, Reno US, under the supervision of Dr. Sushil J. Louis. My research interests include Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI), Machine Learning, and Multi-objective Optimization. I earned my Master’s in Electrical Engineering from the National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra, India (2011–2013), and my Bachelor’s in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Uttar Pradesh Technical University, Lucknow, India (2007–2011).
